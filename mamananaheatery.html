Our Signature Hausa Delicacies

Taste the tradition in every meal, prepared with authentic ingredients.

Tuwo Shinkafa

A staple swallow dish, often paired with Miyan Kuka or Miyan Taushe. Rich and satisfying.

Masa (Rice Cakes)

Soft, sweet, and fluffy rice cakes, perfect as a snack, breakfast, or light meal.

A refreshing, traditional drink made from fermented millet and thick local yogurt. A must-try!
